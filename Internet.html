What is the internet?




The internet is huge global network which made up of many protocols that help to move information.
The internet connects computers all over the world using protocols which are IP, TCP, UDP,and HTTP. Through the internet, people can share information and communication with each other wherever and whenever they are in.
Without internet, people can't do whatever they want to do.
Even if we order something, we text to someone, we send a email, or we search something, we use internet.
Now, internet is indispensable to our life.
